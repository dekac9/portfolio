About me

My journey started 1977 in Belgrade. Finished elementary and high school Petar Drapsin in Belgrade. After that, graduated from the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering in 2002 in Belgrade. 2003 started to work in Belgrade Waterworks as a leading dispatcher in the control and management center. In 2018, promoted to head of the same center. 2020. once more I was promoted to coordinator of the Controll and management Sector. From 2019 till 2020 I represented Belgrade Waterworks team in the FOPIP project (Energy efficiency) sponzored by EU and leaded by Budapest Waterworks. In the bigining of 2021. became a member of Belgrade Waterworks loss reduction team. In march 2021 I've started my Front-end development. Besides English I'm speaking Hungarian but unfortunately, not as good as I want to, but hey, I'm still improving.
